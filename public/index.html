About Bibot Bike Shop

Welcome to Bibot Bike Shop — your one-stop destination for all things cycling. Founded with a passion for two wheels and the freedom they bring, our shop is dedicated to serving riders of all levels. Whether you're a commuter, a weekend explorer, or a competitive cyclist, we offer a carefully selected range of bikes, gear, and accessories to meet your every need.

Our team is made up of experienced cyclists who not only sell bikes but ride them too — so we understand what makes a great ride. From expert repairs to personalized recommendations, we're here to keep you rolling smoothly. Visit us in-store or online and discover how we can help you ride better, safer, and happier.
